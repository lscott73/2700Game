Hero Card Game

Welcome to the Hero Card Game! Here's how to play:

Click on "Draw Cards Player 1" and "Draw Cards Player 2" to draw three cards for each player. Each card has a point value based on its rank (2-10, J, Q, K, A). The player with the highest total points gets a hero assigned based on the point system. Click on "Reveal Winner" to see the result of the battle. The player with the stronger hero wins the round! Click on "Restart Game" to play again.
